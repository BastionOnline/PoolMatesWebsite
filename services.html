Pool Services

We're with you every step of the way

Pre-Season (Spring/Early Summer)

  • Pool Openings: Preparing the pool for use, including cleaning, filling, and balancing chemicals.
  • Safety Cover Removal: Removing and storing the safety cover to get the pool ready for the season.
  • New Liners/Coping Installation: Replacing old liners or coping to refresh the pool’s look and functionality.
  • Equipment Installation: Setting up or replacing equipment like pumps, filters, and heaters before the season starts.

Mid-Season (Summer)

  • Weekly Service/Vacuum Calls: Regular cleaning, balancing chemicals, and vacuuming to keep the pool in top condition.
  • Vacation Vacuum Calls: Maintenance services provided while the pool owner is away.
  • Equipment Repair: Addressing any issues with pool equipment that arise during the season.
  • Heater Service (Licensed TSSA Gas Technician): Ensuring the pool heater is functioning properly, especially if the pool is being used in cooler weather.
  • Service Calls (Troubleshooting and Repairs): Responding to unexpected issues or repairs needed during the season.
  • Leak Detection: Identifying and fixing any leaks that may develop during the pool’s use.

Post-Season (Late Summer/Fall)

  • Pool Closings: Preparing the pool for the off-season, including cleaning, lowering the water level, adding winterizing chemicals, and covering the pool.
  • Safety Cover Installation: Placing a safety cover over the pool to protect it during the off-season.
  • End-of-Season Equipment Maintenance: Servicing equipment to ensure it’s stored properly and ready for the next season.

Off-Season (Winter)

  • Winter Equipment Storage: Storing equipment safely to prevent damage during the cold months.
  • Off-Season Leak Detection: Checking for leaks that might have developed during the off-season due to temperature changes or other factors.
